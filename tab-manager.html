Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 26 Mar 2025

Introduction

Thank you for using our Chrome extension. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data our extension accesses and how it is used.

What Data Do We Access?

Our extension only accesses the following information from your active browser tabs:

  • Host name (domain of the website)
  • Page title
  • Tab index (position of the tab in your browser)

This information is only used to group/rearrange your tabs, assign title to groups and move groups around based on tab position.

What Data Do We Store?

We do not store, save, or send any data outside of your browser. The tab information is only used temporarily while the extension is active.

Do We Share Data?

No, we do not collect, store, or share any user data with third parties.

Permissions

The extension requires permission to access your tabs. This is necessary to organize and group them, but no personal or sensitive data is collected.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If there are significant changes, we will update the date above.
